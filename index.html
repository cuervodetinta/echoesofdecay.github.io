Echoes of Decay

A single-player exploration RPG about uncovering the cause and cure of a dying world.

Play the Game

About the Game

You awaken in a forgotten underground temple with no memory of how you arrived. As you explore interconnected chambers carved in stone, you uncover fragments of a dying world and the truth behind its decay.

Progress through exploration, survive hostile encounters using spear-based combat and elemental abilities, and prepare for the final challenge that awaits deeper within the ruins.

Key Features

  • Exploration-based progression through interconnected temple levels
  • Simple, readable combat with melee and ranged spear attacks
  • Earth-based elemental special ability
  • Environmental puzzles with switches and pressure pads
  • Optional journal entries that reveal lore and foreshadow the final boss
